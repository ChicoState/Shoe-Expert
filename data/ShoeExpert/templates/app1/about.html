Description of Features

Cushioning

Plush means the shoe is meant to feel soft when running. Balanced means the shoe is more responsive and provides greater energy return.


Flexibility

Ability of the sole to bend.


Heel to Toe Drop

Difference between forefoot height and heel height. A shoe with a lower value will be closer to flat and feel more like running barefoot.


Strike Pattern

Strike pattern describes the where the foot hits the ground during a run. Different shoes provide different support for different kinds of strike patterns
